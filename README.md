# PowerBI-Projects
## Project 1-Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI) 
This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze overall business performance.

## Key KPIs
The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:
Total Orders,
Average Sales,
Average Profit,
Average Quantity,
Average Discount.

## Dashboard Features & Visuals
--Trend Analysis
Profit by Quarter (Line Chart)
Total Revenue by Year

--Category Analysis
Average Sales, Profit, Quantity by Category

--Geographic Analysis

--Top 5 States by Profit

--Bottom 5 States by Profit

--Decomposition Tree → enables root cause analysis

## Key Insights
--Sales volume is high, but average profit is relatively low
--Sales are growing year-over-year, but profit growth is inconsistent
--States like California and New York are high-performing
--States like Texas and Ohio are generating losses

## Business Impact
This dashboard enables:

--better decision-making through data visualization
--identification of profitable and loss-making areas
--targeted regional and category-level optimization


## Project 2-Automobile Analysis Dashboard (Power BI) 
This project presents an interactive Automobile Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze overall performance.

## Key KPIs
The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:
--Sales Contribution per category 
--Country wise sales 
--Total Sales 
--Unique customers 
--Last 12 months sales 
--Average 3 months sales 
--Sales YTD 
--Total Profit Margin 
--Total Cost Price 
--Total Sale price 
--Total Spare Parts

## Dashboard Features & Visuals
--Stacked bar chart- Sales price by country 
--Clustered column chart-Sales Price and Profit by Year, profit margin by month, sale price by quarter 
--Funnel chart-Profit by Make 
--Line Charts -Sale price by Year, Sale price by Month 
--Scatter Plot -Sale price and profit by make and model 
--Pie Chart -Sales price by vehicle type 
--Decomposition Tree- Sale Price Drill Down>year>quarter>colour>make 
--Tables-Sales price ranking, year wise sales, top 5 products, monthly average sales, sales price by country and make, MOM 
--Growth, quarter to quarter growth 
--Waterfall chart-Sale price by colour and country name

## Key Insights
--Strong growth in sales over the years. 
--The United Kingdom and United States are the strongest markets. 
--Aston Martin and Rolls-Royce are the top revenue-generating brands. 
--Certain brands and vehicle types require improvement due to lower sales or profit margins. 
--The dashboard helps management identify trends, improve decision-making, and monitor business performance 
effectively. 
--Overall, the project demonstrates how business intelligence tools can transform raw automobile sales data into 
meaningful insights for strategic planning and operational improvement

## Business Impact
The project is based on creating an interactive automobile sales dashboard in Power BI Desktop to analyse vehicle sales, 
profit, customer trends, country performance, and product performance.
--Monitor total automobile sales and profit. 
--Compare sales across countries, years, months, and quarters. 
--Identify top-performing vehicle brands and models. 
--Track profit margins and growth trends. 
--Analyse customer and product contribution. 
--Support business decision-making using visual reports.
